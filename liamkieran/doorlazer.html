Door Lazer

Research

Before this project, neither of us had much knowledge on wiring ultrasonic sensors. We used examples on schoology for setting up different sensors. We also used teachwithict.com to help us as there was a guide on how to set up a microbit to an ultrasonic sensor. We found both of these resources extremely helpful for our project. We also consulted with other members of of our class who were doing similar projects with ultrasonic sensors.

Response to brief

Our project is a pair of sesnsors that when placed on a door, will detect whether someone entered or exited the building. When one of the sensors is activated, it will create a "beep" noise alerting the front desk that someone has entered or left the building. We decided to use a microbit rather than a raspberry pi as we only needed to play a note with the speaker. If we wanted to play a sound file, we would have used a raspberry pi. The microbirt was also more simple to programme as we could write block code on makecode.org

Issues and bug fixes

At first we were aiming the sensor at the empty room and it was giving inconsistant results. We put a board up so there was a constant signal to the sensor. This gave us more accurate results when we got in the path of the sensor.

Evaluation

Overall, we are extremely happy with the final result of our project. It serves the purpose that we had intended it to and and works at least 90% of the time from our tests. After all bug fixes, we have gotten it to work almost exactly as planned.
